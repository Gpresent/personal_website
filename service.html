Engineers Without Borders: FIELD

In the spring semester of my freshman year, I got involved with the Texas A&M's FIELD branch of Engineers Without Borders. Our goal was to build a structurally sound pergola on the FIELD property, which is in Bryan, TX. FIELD helps bridge the gap between new and experienced EWB members while building projects that help the Bryan and College Station communities.

BUILD Tamu Volunteering

In the fall semester of my sophomore year, I volunteered with BUILD, one of Texas A&M's most renouned service organizations. BUILD's mission is to build medical clinics out of shipping containers and send them to areas of the world that don't have access to good medical care. During my volunteering, I mainly helped attach interior walls in one of the clinics under construction.
